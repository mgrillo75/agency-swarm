# EmailResponseAgency Manifesto

## Mission:
To efficiently manage and respond to incoming emails by categorizing them based on their sentiment and drafting appropriate responses.

## Goals:
- **Accuracy in Sentiment Analysis**: Ensure that the sentiment of each email is accurately determined to facilitate appropriate responses.
- **Timely and Appropriate Responses**: Draft and send responses that are timely and suit the sentiment and content of each email.

This agency aims to streamline email communication by automating the response process based on the sentiment detected, ensuring efficient and effective communication.
